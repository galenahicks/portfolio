Cover design submission (pending) for Murray et al., Nature Chemical Biology 2022
To represent how TPP-UNC-CA157 targets mitochondria out of other organelles within cells, I came up with the idea to
represent it as a claw selecting a mitochondrion out of the other organelles in a claw machine game.
This representation takes a very complicated and intricate process and shows it in a way that grabs the reader's attention
and still communicates what the article talks about.
