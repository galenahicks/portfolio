Metropolitan Opera: Live in HD

For this project, I was tasked with creating animated posters that play in movie theaters around New York promoting the Met's Live in HD Series of opera broadcasts. In order for the animations to be a seamless loop, I created a design that resembles a "Dolly Zoom" or "Hitchcock Shot", to exemplify the cinematic nature of the Live in HD broadcasts. I also wanted to create a design that works was easily translated for multiple productions, as this template is used for each opera in theaters that year.

Looping movie posters for the Met: Live in HD movie theater screenings, Grounded, 2024-25 Season, Fidelio
