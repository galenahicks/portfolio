Hi! My name is Galen Aubrey Hicks, and I'm a designer with a background in digital, print and motion design across arts and visual arts institutions. I am personally interested in combining design with sound, audial art and music.

I graduated with a BFA in Communication Design from Washington University in St. Louis, and am currently a graphic designer at the Metropolitan Opera. I have previously worked in design with the Pagliarini Lab and in social media with SFMOMA.
Freelance clients include National Theatre in America.
