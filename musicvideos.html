Midtown Market

Video for my song "Midtown Market" edited by me and co-directed by Ahmed Motiwala and I. Growing up in midtown Memphis, we shot around different midtown landmarks that I frequented growing up (many referenced in the song). We shot many of the scenes with a tape camcorder to emphasize the nostalgia these places hold and used glitchy and surreal editing to show how these places blend in together in my memory.
