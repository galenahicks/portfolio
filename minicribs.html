SFMOMA: Mini Cribs

These are some of the assets I created for a new series "Mini Cribs", a collaboration between the social media and interpretative media teams at SFMOMA. The series is based on the "Cribs" series that lives on the SFMOMA YouTube channel, where we get in-depth looks at artists' studios and workspaces; Mini Cribs does this, but takes a bit more of an informal approach and is filmed with a social-first priority.
